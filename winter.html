There is a sharp, undeniable clarity to a winter ride. The air is freezing, biting at the edges, but the second the tires grip the asphalt, everything goes warm. It’s the thrill of cutting through a misty morning, the engine humming against the quiet cold, and the solid, reassuring weight of her holding on tight. The world around us is frozen in time, but we are pure, unstoppable motion.
