“

I'd climb a thousand mountains if it meant getting to the top with you.

— a promise, not a metaphor

My legs were shaking before we even hit the second switchback, and you laughed at me the whole way up — but you also slowed down without ever making it obvious, so I wouldn't fall behind. That's sort of how you do everything: pushing me higher while somehow making it feel easy. When we finally reached the top and the whole valley opened up beneath us, I didn't even look at the view for the first few minutes. I was too busy looking at you.
